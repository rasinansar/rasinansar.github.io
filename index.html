About BSM

Founded in United Arab Emirates in 2000, BSM Trading has grown into one of the most trusted international trading company in Middle East. Drawing on decades of expertise in food & nonfood products, as well as LP Gas (cooking & fuel for warmers) & hygienic products. BSM excels at efficiently delivering high quality products in order to maximize the client profile.

Efficiency & perfection is our motto

BSM has kept its values of trust, reliability, and efficiency even as it has expanded. Now, having grown into a respected international trade company, BSM uses the latest logistics technologies, real-time pricing, international tracking services, and a broad network of worldwide suppliers in order to offer maximum value and great quality products to its clients.
